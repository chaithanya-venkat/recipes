Maggi Maggi Maggi

Ingredients

  • Maggi
  • Soy sauce
  • Schezwan sauce
  • Masala E Magic
  • Cheese
  • Spring onion

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add Maggi masala e magic, soy sauce and schezwan sauce
  2. Heat 3 tbsp of oil until hot
  3. Pour hot oil on the mixture and stir well
  4. cook maggi as instructed in the packet
  5. Drain the water and add the noodles to the sauce mixture
  6. Mix noodles thoroughly until coated
  7. Grate cheese and garnish with spring onions
  8. Enjoy!
